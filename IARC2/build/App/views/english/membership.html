Membership

The membership fee will be used to finance the association's services that are provided to its members.
The membership fee is collected on a calendar basis [ending every 31.12] and their payment at the beginning of each activity year is of crucial importance for the purpose of planning the annual activity budget.

Membership fee for foreign residents

  • 100USD for three years

Membership fees for Israeli residents

  • 250NIS per person for renewal of membership until 28.2 of the calendar year (ending on 31.12)
  • 300NIS starting from 1.3 for the year of activity ending on 31.12.

Club and family stations

  • 300NIS per person for renewal of membership until 28.2 of the calendar year (ending on 31.12)
  • 350NIS starting from 1.3 for the year of activity ending on 31.12.

Youth and soldiers

  • 150NIS per person for renewal of membership until 28.2 of the calendar year (ending on 31.12)
  • 200NIS starting from 1.3 for the year of activity ending on 31.12.

New amateurs who join the association as of 1.10 for the calendar year of activity (ending on 31.12) will also receive full membership for the calendar year of activity that follows as part of the payment made.

The membership fee can be paid in a number of ways:

  • Credit Card
  • To pay for the PAYBOX app: Use the following link: https://payboxapp.page.link/NfSJCpuQZf6VELdM8
    You can pay as a guest - no need to install an app. Fill in the required details including the call sign.
  • Face to face with the treasurer at a meeting at the association's offices: 3 Alpert Street, Yehud, or at a meeting that the association holds from time to time.
  • Check by mail (not by registered mail) to the association's offices Address: Israel Amateur Radio Association (PO Box) PO Box 17600 Tel Aviv Zip code 6117501 Be sure to write the call sign on the back of the check!
  • Bank transfer to the association's account:
    If you have chosen a bank transfer, be sure to write down the amateur's call sign in the details in order to facilitate the management of the registration and the link between the amount transferred and the paying amateur.
