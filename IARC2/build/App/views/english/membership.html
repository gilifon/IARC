Membership

Every licensed ham of age 17 or more is eligible of membership in IARC.
Additionally, the members committee can approve admission in a state of "friendship". An IARC friend can be for example someone who is not 17 years old yet, or is not a citizen of the state of Israel, or is a club station, or a corporate. A friend is eligible of most of members' privileges, notably except the rights to elect or be elected to IARC's institutes.
For enrolling, either contact us, or by email at membership@iarc.org
For any question about membership fees, you can also contact the treasurer at treasurer@iarc.org

Membership fees for Israeli residents

Membership fees for Israeli residents are:

  • 250 NIS for a person
  • 300 NIS for families and club stations
  • 150 NIS for Youth and Israeli troops

Paying the membership fees can be done either in person (by arriving to the IARC offices or by meeting the treasurer), by mail (by sending a cheque) or by Bank transfer.

Membership fees for non-Israeli residents

Membership fees for non-Israeli residents are currently at 30 USD per year.
